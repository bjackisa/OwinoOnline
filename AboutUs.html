What is Owino Online?

Owino online is an online market place established in 2021. Even though the invention hasn't yet been incoporated in Uganda, it aims at keeping St. Balikudembe Market populary Owino open to all clients even during lockdowns on health cause like one by establishment of this project. It is also aimed at reducing over crowding in Uganda's biggest Marketplace by more than 65 per cent which could create more ease in work, reduce on crimes like theft,and increase health safety. Founded by Jackisa Daniel Barack and Hakim Kasasa Senfuma, and Directed by Tracy Muzaki who are all Computer Science Students from UTAMU the country's fastest growing "MIT like" institution.
Owino Online collects together different vendors mainly with second hand fabrics and textlie materials to sell online, carrying out logistics of purchases and guess what; We'll soon accept online payment! with mobile transactions methods, credit cards, and Bitcoin.
